Matured cohort
Originations old enough for first-payment delinquency (FPD) to be observed reliably — i.e. the first scheduled payment has come due and enough time has passed to see whether the borrower reached 4+, 7+, 15+, or 30+ DPD on that payment.
UK filter: captures in credit_first_pmt_dq with DT < today − 30 days (aligned to context §2.3 IS_MATURED_FOR_FPDQX_READING). Current book: 415,212 loans · £56.6M origination £.
Why exclude immature loans? Recent captures would dilute FPDQ rates downward — they are still inside their first payment cycle. That is why May-26 FPDQ30 may show “—” while FPDQ4 is available: longer maturity is required for 30+ DPD first-payment reads.
Formula (§2.3): FPDQ4$% = Σ(first-payment DQ4+ £) ÷ Σ(matured origination £). Each FPDQ step (4 / 7 / 15 / 30) uses its own maturity flag in the official framework.
Fraud: Matured cohort here is also ex confirmed fraud (FRAUD_IND = 0 on credit_first_pmt_dq, per §FPD2).
